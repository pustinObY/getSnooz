Shipping Info

We're thrilled to offer free shipping on all orders! If you need your SNOOZ in a hurry, we also offer 1-Business-Day and 2-Business-Day shipping options for an additional fee (contiguous United States only, please allow for up to one business day for order processing).


Shipping Time Frames (Contiguous U.S.)

  • Fast(ish): 5-7 Business Days
  • Faster: 2 Business Days (if ordered by 1:00pm ET)
  • Fastest: 1 Business Day (if ordered by 1:00pm ET)

Shipping Time Frames for Alaska, Hawaii, Guam, Puerto Rico, American Samoa, & U.S. APO/FBO

  • Standard: 7-10 Business Days (please allow up to 21 days for U.S. APO/FBO)

 

Do you ship internationally?

SNOOZ is currently available only in the U.S. Please to subscribe to our mailing list at the bottom of this page for updates!


Will I receive tracking information?

We’ll send an email confirmation with tracking information when your order ships.


Where are you shipping from?

We are based in Las Vegas, NV, but our inventory is spread across fulfillment centers nationwide in order to reduce transit times.


How will SNOOZ be shipped?

We use multiple carriers for fulfillment including (USPS, UPS, FedEx, and DHL). Unfortunately, we’re not able to control the shipping carrier used in any one shipment.
